Light of Love

See the unseen. Embrace the forgotten. Love without limits.

Light of Love is a non-profit organization that is motivated and sustained by the love of Jesus Christ. As a ministry team that has been impacted by sex trafficking, whether it be our own personal testimony or the testimony of others, we are moved by the harsh realities that exist in our city today. As a team we share the same motivation and that is to bring the love of Christ to those individuals that are still in the life.
